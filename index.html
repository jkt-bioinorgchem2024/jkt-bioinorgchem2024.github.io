It is our great pleasure to invite you to Japan-Korea-Taiwan Bioinorganic Chemistry Symposium 2024, which will be held on July 17-18, 2024 in Sapporo, Japan. We hope that you join us as an invited speaker and share us your exciting science.

About your talk

Each talk is scheduled for 30 minutes including 5 minutes for questions.

About Abstract and CV

We kindly request that you prepare an abstract and CV. Although this request was not mentioned in our last e-mail. The template and example are attached for your convenience. Please submit the document by 30th June.

Accommodation

Your accommodation has been reserved from 16th-19th for three nights at Washington Hotel Plaza Sapporo near Sapporo station, just 2-minutes walk away.

For more information, please visit: https://washington.jp/en/sapporo/

Please note that we confirm some guests stay shorter or use other accommodation.

From the New-Chitose airport to Sapporo station

It takes approximately 30 minutes by JR train from New-Chitose airport to Sapporo city center. The JR train is the easiest direct option available. Please be sure to get of the train in Sapporo station. Shin-Sapporo is a different station.

For more details, please visit: https://www.new-chitose-airport.jp/en/access/jr/

Eve event

The evening event will held at “Hoheikan” starting at 18:00 on 16th July. To reach the venue, take the subway Namboku Line from Sapporo Station to Nakajima Koen Station, followed by a five-minute walk. We will gather in the hotel beforehand and depart together. If you anticipate arriving at the hotel after 17:00, please inform us in advance.

For further information, please visit: https://www.s-hoheikan.jp/en/

Access to Hokkaido Univ.

The symposium will be hosted in Global Research Center for Food & Medical Innovation (FMI), in Hokkaido Univ. The campus is within walking distance from Sapporo Station, and there are also bus services available on campus. Japanese attendees will accompany you.

For details, please visit:

https://www.fmi.hokudai.ac.jp/en/access/

https://www.global.hokudai.ac.jp/about/access-maps/

The symposium will span two full days on 17th and 18th July.

On the 17th, the session will start from 9:00 to 17:30, followed by a banquet at the university restauraunt at 18:30.

For more details about the restaurant, please visit: https://cafedegohan.com/

On the 18th, the sessions will start from 9:30 to 17:30. At 18:30, we will have a Genghis Khan (Japanese Mutton BBQ) dinner at the Sapporo Beer Garden. The bus leaves from the symposium hall at 18:00.

For more information, please visit: https://www.sapporo-bier-garten.jp/global/english.html

The program will be updated in the symposium website within a week. Please feel free to ask if you have any questions.

We are looking forward to meeting you soon in Sapporo!
